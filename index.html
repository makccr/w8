W8 - Potential Lost to Compound Growth Calculator

The World's Ulimate Bad Spending Habit Breaker

Tired of spending that $10 bucks on a cheese-burger for no reason? Or $4 bucks on the white monster, or fancy coffee? The W8 calculator shows you precisely how much that habit is costing you.

Info

The W8 calculator assumes that the value inputed here is a legitimate habitiual bad spending habit. The aim of the caluclator is to show you the potential losses that would be accrued by wasting the money rather than investing it. The input assumes a bi-daily habit, and multiplies input by 178 (every-other day), before calculting compound interest from an investment. As of Decmeber 2024 the S&P 500 returned an average of 10.985% a year, or, when adjusted for inflation 8.262%. These percentages assume that any dividends are automatically re-invested into the same index fund. Our calculations use the inflation adjusted value of 8.262%.
